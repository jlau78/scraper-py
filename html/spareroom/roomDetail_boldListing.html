After 3 fantastic years our beloved Tabs and Ollie have purchased their own home and are moving out. This leaves our top floor ensuite room vacant. It is a stunning, light and airy top floor room with ample space, a king size bed and a large ensuite with shower.

The home itself is on Hamilton Road and is lovely and spacious. We are roughly between Dollis Hill Station and Gladstone Park (2 minutes walk to either)

In the house we have a small spare room for visitors, superfast wifi and a decent TV package. The pictures show more or less everything but there is also a downstairs toilet and utility room. Gardens front and back.

About us: Jack (29) works in education and is currently studying for a masters alongside his usual work. I'm Mike (28) and I work in the City. Myself and Jack are old friends from Ireland and we moved to London 6 or so years ago. We have busy work and social lives and are always partial to a pint or two on the weekend but are very tame midweek.

Bills - Monthly Estimates Per Person:

Council Tax: £55.50
TV and Wifi: £10
Water: £9
Electricity: £28

Please do send us a paragraph or two about yourself/yourselves if you are interested.

***Ideal for Couples***

We are anticipating a lot of interest but will get back to you when we can.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best wishes,

Mike
